our story

Summit Seltzer Company is the first tap room to exclusively offer its own in-house fermented and produced draft hard seltzer. It’s like a brewery, but for hard seltzer— a Seltzery! We will offer our homemade seltzer drafts, as well as seltzer based cocktails, in an open, airy, and inviting environment.

Our motto, “Take it to the Top” emphasizes the Summit Seltzer Co. way of life: active, healthy, and united with local community. Summit focuses on taking life to its full potential (to the top); in our workouts, in our daily routines, and in our choice of beverage. Summit Seltzer Co. fills the gap that exists between active consumers and a light, low calorie/carb, refreshing alcoholic beverage option.

Come enjoy our tap room, seltzery, event space, and climbing wall Fall 2019!

-Kristin Cagney, Founder
